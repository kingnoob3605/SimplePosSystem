How to use
TindaPOS

Your simple, mobile-first point-of-sale for street vendors, milk tea shops, and small food businesses.

Get Started →

1

First-Time Setup

Before you start selling, set up your business info and payment options in the Settings tab (⚙️ bottom-right).

1
Enter your business name Your store name appears at the top of every screen and on receipts.
2
Upload your GCash QR code Screenshot your QR from the GCash app, upload it here. You can crop it to fit perfectly.
3
Add your categories e.g. Drinks 🥤, Snacks 🍟, Milk Tea 🧋 — these filter your items while selling.
4
Add items to your menu Go to the Menu tab (📋) and tap the + button to add your first item.
💡
TindaPOS works fully offline — no internet required during selling. Your data stays on the device.
2

Managing Your Menu

Go to the Menu tab (📋) to add, edit, or delete items.

1
Tap the + button Opens a form to create a new item. Fill in the name and price.
2
Add a photo or emoji Upload a product photo or pick an emoji icon to represent the item on the sell screen.
3
Add sizes / variants (optional) e.g. Small ₱65, Medium ₱75, Large ₱90. When variants exist, tapping the item opens a size picker.
4
Add add-ons (optional) e.g. Extra Pearls +₱10, Cream Cheese +₱15. These appear as tappable chips in checkout.
5
Enable stock tracking (optional) Enter a stock count and toggle Track Stock on. The count decrements automatically after each sale.

3

Taking an Order

The Sell tab (🛒) is where you work during service. Tap items to add them to the cart.

1
Filter by category Use the pill tabs at the top to quickly jump between Drinks, Food, Snacks, etc.
2
Tap an item to add it Items with sizes open a picker first. Plain items go straight into the cart.
3
View and edit your cart Tap the total bar at the bottom (▲) to expand the cart. Use the big − and + buttons to adjust quantities.
4
Tap "Bayad na →" to checkout Opens the checkout sheet with the full order summary.

The cart bar shows a badge with the item count. Expand it to see the full order and remove items with the big − button.
4

Checkout Process

The checkout sheet shows your order summary, payment options, and calculates change automatically.

1
Review the order All items, add-ons, and special requests are listed. Tap add-on chips to add/remove them. Tap ✏️ to add a note (e.g. "less ice").
2
Apply a discount (optional) Tap the "🏷️ Diskwento" link. Choose flat ₱ or % and enter the amount. The total updates live.
3
Choose Cash or GCash For Cash: enter the amount received or tap a denomination chip (₱100, ₱500…). Change is shown automatically.
4
Tap the confirm button For GCash: your QR code is shown full-screen for the customer to scan. Tap "Paid" when done.
5
Digital receipt appears A receipt screen shows the full order, total, and change. Tap "Done" to return to selling.

5

Viewing Sales Reports

The Report tab (📊) shows your sales data for today, this week, or this month.

1
Switch time periods Tap Today / Linggo / Buwan tabs at the top to switch between daily, weekly, and monthly views.
2
See total revenue + breakdown The summary card shows total transactions. Below it, Cash vs GCash split cards appear.
3
Check Best Sellers A ranked list of your top items by quantity sold, with total revenue per item.
4
Tap any transaction to expand it See the full itemized order, add-ons, special requests, and discount applied.
5
Export to CSV Tap the "📥 Export CSV" button in the header to download your sales data as a spreadsheet.
6

Shift Management

Track your cash float at the start of each shift and get a summary when you close.

1
Open a shift Go to Settings → tap "Open Shift". Enter the starting cash in your drawer (e.g. ₱500 for change).
2
Sell normally throughout the day All transactions during the open shift are tracked automatically.
3
Close the shift at end of day Tap "Close Shift" to see your summary: cash sales, GCash sales, total, and expected cash in drawer.
💡
Expected Cash = Opening Float + Cash Sales. Count your actual drawer and compare to catch any discrepancies.

Features at a Glance

Everything TindaPOS can do — built for Filipino street vendors and milk tea shops.

🛒

Fast Selling

Tap items to add, expand cart for big ± buttons, charge in seconds.

🧋

Variants & Sizes

Small / Medium / Large with different prices per variant.

✨

Add-ons

Extra Pearls, Cream Cheese, Whipped Cream — tappable chips at checkout.

✏️

Order Notes

Per-item special requests — "less ice", "no sugar", "extra spicy".

🏷️

Discounts

Flat ₱ or percentage % discount applied at checkout.

💚

GCash QR

Upload your QR once. Full-screen display for customers to scan.

🖨️

Receipt Printer

ESC/POS thermal printer via USB (Chrome/Edge desktop only).

📦

Stock Tracking

Optional per-item stock count, auto-decremented after each sale.

📊

Sales Reports

Today, weekly, monthly — with best sellers and CSV export.

🕐

Shift Management

Open with a cash float, close for a full shift summary.

📲

PWA / Installable

Add to Home Screen on Android Chrome for a native app feel.

🔒

Offline First

No internet needed. All data stored locally on your device.

⚠️
Receipt printer (Web Serial API) only works on Chrome or Edge on a desktop/laptop. It is not supported on Safari or mobile browsers.
